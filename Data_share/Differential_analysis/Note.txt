File name format:

1) Differential expressed genes/Differetial open regions between every two adjacent time points:

text format tables are included.
W/A_time1_vs_time2 (A means Adapted (Tibetan), W means wildtype (Han))
Values 1 means time 1> time2, while -1 means time1 < time2;															


2) Differential expressed genes/Differetial open regions between Tibetan and Han across all time points

xlsx format tables are included.
T-test with and without FDR p-values are provided.
Direction(-1 means W<A, 1 means W>A; A(Tibetan), W(Han)).
